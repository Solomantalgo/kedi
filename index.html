Where Tradition Meets Innovation

The Adungu Cultural Center is a visionary, dynamic, inclusive cultural, community empowerment and creative hub located in the heart of Gulu City, Northern Uganda. Named after the traditional Adungu instrument, we symbolize the fusion of indigenous heritage and forward-looking innovation fit for the 21st-century needs.

We are a beacon of cultural pride, economic empowerment, healing, and artistic expression—with a special focus on youth and women as primary agents of transformation.

Cultural Heritage Preserved

Our Vision

To be a leading center for cultural renaissance, economic opportunity, and creative innovation in Northern Uganda and across the East African region.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote indigenous cultures while empowering youth and women through skills development, creative expression, education, and enterprise creation.

Strategic Objectives

Rooted in the rich cultural legacy of Northern Uganda, the Center addresses both the socio-economic needs and the creative aspirations of the community.

1

Preserve and promote Acholi and Northern Uganda's indigenous knowledge systems, languages, and artistic traditions.

2

Facilitate skills development and employment for youth and women through the cultural and creative industries.

3

Provide a safe, nurturing space for healing, learning, and intergenerational dialogue.

4

Foster cultural tourism, local pride, and global engagement through festivals and residencies.

5

Use technology and innovation to digitize, disseminate, and monetize cultural content.

Our Cultural Programs

Eight transformative programs that blend traditional wisdom with modern innovation, creating pathways for cultural preservation and community empowerment.

Cultural Heritage & Indigenous Knowledge

The heart of our center, focusing on safeguarding and transmitting traditional knowledge systems while creating interactive learning spaces.

  • Weekly storytelling sessions with community elders
  • Traditional healing & herbal medicine workshops
  • Cultural cuisine hub managed by local women
  • Oral history & cultural museum with rotating exhibits
Learn More

Performing Arts & Visual Expression

Nurturing artistic excellence while celebrating Uganda's diverse musical and visual heritage through training and performance.

  • Fully equipped cultural music recording studio
  • Weekly cultural nights featuring traditional dances
  • Art & craft village with workshops and studios
  • Art therapy programs for trauma-affected individuals
Learn More

Film, Media & Digital Learning

Empowering youth and creatives to become content creators, documentarians, and cultural archivists using digital technology.

  • Three-month film school courses in documentary making
  • Radio and podcast studio for cultural programming
  • Digital cultural archive using mobile and VR technology
  • Training in cultural content monetization
Learn More

Women & Girls Empowerment

Positioning women and girls as central to societal transformation through economic, leadership, and cultural empowerment.

  • Ghetto mothers empowerment hub with skills training
  • Girls' soccer academy with leadership mentorship
  • Annual Girls & Culture Cup multi-day event
  • Cooperative savings schemes and business support
Learn More

Green Culture & Innovation

Linking culture with ecology and digital creativity through sustainability practices rooted in traditional knowledge.

  • Live herbal garden with medicinal plants
  • Environmental & climate storytelling training
  • Youth innovation corner with tech tools
  • Permaculture and climate-resilient agriculture
Learn More

Festivals, Residencies & Outreach

Expanding impact through public events, national partnerships, and mobile cultural programming across the region.

  • Annual Adungu Festival with thousands of visitors
  • Cultural exchange residencies for artists and scholars
  • Mobile cultural caravan to remote villages
  • Traveling exhibitions and school programs
Learn More

Sports, Wellness & Leadership

Using athletics to build resilience, teamwork, and leadership while promoting community health and unity.

  • Basketball development hub with courts and training
  • Health & fitness education workshops
  • Inclusive sports events for youth with disabilities
  • Leadership bootcamps through sport activities
Learn More

Product Development & Market Access

Transforming local raw products into branded, value-added goods that reflect Northern Uganda's cultural identity.

  • Value addition workshops for local produce
  • Packaging & branding studio with design training
  • Marketing & trade linkages to multiple channels
  • E-commerce and cooperative management training
Learn More
